How to Add Fitness Data

Apple Health (Apple Watch, Oura)

Export from iPhone: Health app > Profile > Export All Health Data

datahub import apple-health export.xml

Peloton

Configure your credentials and sync

datahub sync peloton

Tonal

Configure your credentials and sync strength workouts

datahub config tonal.email you@email.com datahub sync tonal

Oura Ring

Get your token from cloud.ouraring.com/personal-access-tokens

datahub sync oura
